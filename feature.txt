Add featurs to make a calculator 
1. **Basic Calculation**
### Perform fundamental arithmetic operations:
- Addition (`+`)
- Subtraction (`-`)
- Multiplication (`*`)
- Division (`/`)

2. **Calculate the Power of a Number**
### Compute the power of any given number:
- Formula: `base^exponent`
- Example: `2^3 = 8`

3. **Solve Equations with Variables** 
### Solve algebraic equations involving variables:
- Supports linear equations `ax + b = c`
- Quadratic equations `ax^2 + bx + c = 0`
